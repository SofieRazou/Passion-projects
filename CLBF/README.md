## Control Lyapunov & Barrier Function system implementation and intergration with MATLAB, Simulink and C/C++ wrapper
In this project, I endeavoured in contructing the computational logic of a closed-loop control algorithm, assessed on moving boundary conditions of two kinds:
1. Lyapunov stability constraints,
2. Barrier conditions

This formulization of the control as an quadrating optimization problem, found application on a simple state-space described system, in Simulink.
